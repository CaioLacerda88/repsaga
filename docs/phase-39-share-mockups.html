The Bestiary · session-as-conquest

Photo = hero, full-bleed. One generated creature you felled, deterministic from the session. Dominant body part → creature LINE, session XP → TIER (E–S), PR/rank-up → BOSS, 3+ parts → CHIMERA. XP + tonnage shown, plus an achievement phrase from what you did. Cinzel serif for names. Full system in docs/bestiary-spec.md.
